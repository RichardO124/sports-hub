Tennis

A Sport of Precision and Power

Tennis is a fast‑paced sport played all over the world. Whether on grass, clay, or hard courts,

players rely on agility, timing, and strategy to outplay their opponents.

Major tournaments like Wimbledon and the US Open attract millions of fans every year.

Tennis can be played alone or in pairs. Players hit a ball over a net using a racket.
