Basketball

🏀

Basketball is played indoors or outdoors. Players try to throw the ball into a high hoop to score points. The original game had 13 rules, many of which are still used today. Basketball is now played in over 200 countries. 5 players on each team Fast‑moving game Good for fitness and teamwork. Basketball is also known for its fast pace, slam dunks, and incredible athleticism
